Services

Consultative Partnership

Bernadette approaches each vacancy as a consultative partnership, understanding that finding the right client requires more than just matching skills. She focuses on understanding the unique needs of both her clients and candidates, ensuring a perfect match that goes beyond technical skills to include personality and cultural fit.

Tailored Candidate Selection

With a thorough understanding of her clients’ needs and culture, Bernadette ensures that candidates are not only technically qualified but also align with the company’s values. She goes beyond just skills, factoring in personality and cultural compatibility to make the best match.

Thorough Candidate Screening

All candidates are carefully interviewed with high attention to detail. Reference checks are recorded verbatim and included in the CV. On request, further checks such as credit, criminal, and qualifications verifications can be conducted. Credit and criminal checks are automatically performed prior to offer stage for all financially-oriented candidates.
